Why Grievance is important?

  • It provides a mechanism for students to address issues or concerns they may have about their educational experience, ensuring that all students are treated fairly and respectfully..
  • Having a grievance system in place promotes transparency within educational institutions by establishing clear procedures for addressing student complaints
    or grievances..
  • It empowers students to voice their concerns and actively participate in improving the quality of education and campus life
  • Addressing grievances promptly can prevent minor issues from escalating into larger conflicts or legal disputes, ultimately saving time and resources for both students and the institution.

By addressing issues related to harassment, discrimination, or other forms of misconduct, a grievance system contributes to creating a safe and supportive environment conducive to student well-being and academic success.
